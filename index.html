Advanced Strategies to Save on Car Insurance

Maximize your savings with these proven strategies used by insurance experts.

Compare Multiple Quotes

Get quotes from at least 5 different providers. Rates can vary by hundreds of dollars for the same coverage.

Bundle Policies

Combine auto with home or renters insurance for discounts of 10-25% on both policies.

Improve Credit Score

In most states, better credit can significantly reduce your premiums. Check your report for errors.

Defensive Driving Courses

Complete an approved course to qualify for discounts up to 10% with many insurers.

Higher Deductibles

Increasing your deductible from $200 to $500 could reduce your premium by 15-30%.

Drop Unnecessary Coverage

For older cars, consider dropping collision if the premium exceeds 10% of the car's value.
